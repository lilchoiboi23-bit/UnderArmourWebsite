How it works

Shoot more than 105 made threes, record the attempt, and submit the clip with your contact info. Only the first 10 verified entries win.

The contest is built for a clean, simple challenge: make the shots, prove it on video, and claim the prize before the first 10 spots are gone.

Contest Rules

  • Make more than 105 consecutive 3-pointers.
  • Record the full attempt so the count is visible.
  • Email the proof video with your name and contact info.
  • The first 10 verified submissions win $10,000.

Prize Details

  • Each of the first 10 winners gets $10,000.
  • Entries are verified in the order they are received.
  • Proof must show the shot count clearly.

What to submit

  • Your video proof.
  • Your full name.
  • Your email address.
  • A short note with the final count.
