About UA

The pursuit of better.

Under Armour is built around helping athletes perform at their best. The brand's about page centers on innovation, purpose, and a simple idea: make gear that helps people keep pushing forward.

Our vision

Performance solutions that keep raising the bar.

We focus on performance apparel, footwear, and accessories that are made for athletes who want more from every rep, run, and game.

Our company

What makes UA different

Under Armour's company story is about a relentless drive to be better, stronger, and more focused. That shows up in the way the brand talks about innovation, product, and the athletes it serves.

Our purpose

Empowering those who strive for more.

The purpose page on UA's about site leans into athletes, community, sustainability, and equality. The message is simple: build better gear and keep supporting people who are working toward something bigger.

UA's story began with a performance t-shirt and grew into a global sports brand. The about site frames that history around constant improvement and the idea that the work never really stops.
